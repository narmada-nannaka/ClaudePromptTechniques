# Marathon Nutrition Plan

## Daily Calorie & Macronutrient Targets

**Estimated Daily Needs:** 2,400-2,800 calories (training dependent)

### Macronutrient Distribution for Endurance:
- **Carbohydrates: 55-65%** (330-455g/day) — fuel for long runs
- **Protein: 12-15%** (70-105g/day) — recovery & muscle maintenance
- **Fat: 20-25%** (55-75g/day) — sustained energy

---

## Pre-Marathon Carb Loading (3-7 days before)

- **Increase carbs to 70% of calories** (400-500g/day)
- **Reduce fiber & fat** slightly to minimize GI issues
- Focus on easily digestible carbs

---

## Recommended Foods by Category

### **Carbohydrates (Prioritize)**
- Oats, white rice, pasta, bread
- Bananas, dates, honey
- Sports drinks, energy gels
- Bagels, rice cakes

### **Protein**
- Chicken, fish, lean beef
- Eggs, Greek yogurt
- Legumes (lentils, beans)
- Cottage cheese

### **Healthy Fats**
- Olive oil, nuts, avocado
- Fatty fish (salmon)
- Nut butters

### **Hydration**
- 3-4L water daily
- Electrolyte drinks during long runs
- Coconut water

---

## Pre-Race Day (24 hours before)
- Light, familiar, carb-heavy meals
- Avoid high-fiber, high-fat, spicy foods
- Stay well-hydrated

# Vegan Nutrition Plan for Soccer Performance

## Daily Calorie & Macro Target
- **Calories:** ~2,400-2,800 (adjust based on training intensity)
- **Protein:** 110-130g/day (1.5-1.8g per kg)
- **Carbs:** 320-360g/day (4.5-5g per kg)
- **Fat:** 60-80g/day (mostly unsaturated)

## Key Food Categories

### Protein Sources (25-35g per serving)
- Legumes: lentils, chickpeas, black beans, peas
- Tofu & tempeh (15-20g per 100g)
- Hemp/pumpkin seeds
- Nutritional yeast
- Vegan protein powder (pea, hemp, or blended)

### Carbs for Energy & Recovery
- **Whole grains:** quinoa, brown rice, oats, whole wheat pasta
- **Starchy vegetables:** sweet potatoes, regular potatoes
- **Fruits:** bananas (potassium), dates, berries

### Healthy Fats (for inflammation & hormones)
- Nuts: almonds, walnuts, cashews
- Seeds: chia, flax, sunflower
- Avocado, olive oil, coconut oil

### Micronutrients to Prioritize
- **Iron:** fortified cereals, lentils, spinach (pair with vitamin C)
- **B12:** fortified plant milks, nutritional yeast, supplements
- **Omega-3s:** flax seeds, chia seeds, walnuts
- **Calcium:** fortified milk alternatives, tahini, leafy greens
- **Zinc:** hemp seeds, pumpkin seeds, chickpeas

## Sample Day

**Breakfast:** Oatmeal + banana + almond butter + flax seeds

**Pre-Training Snack:** Dates + almonds

**Lunch:** Quinoa + roasted chickpeas + sweet potato + olive oil

**Post-Training:** Protein smoothie (plant milk + protein powder + berries)

**Dinner:** Lentil pasta + tempeh + vegetables + tahini sauce

**Evening Snack:** Hummus + vegetables

---

**Key tip:** Include protein at every meal and prioritize carbs around training times. Consider B12 and vitamin D supplements.
